Transform Your Cash Into Digital Universe

PayNety will launch as innovative payment solution that allows users to make various transactions using cash, such as utility payments, mobile top-ups, insurance fees, money transfers, etc.
Our kiosks, planned for deployment in early 2025, will offer a convenient and accessible way to bridge cash with digital transactions. Currently, we are in the process of securing necessary licenses to ensure full compliance.
